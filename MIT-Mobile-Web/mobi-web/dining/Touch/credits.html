The MIT Mobile Web is developed and maintained by MIT's Information Services & Technology (IS&T) as a service to the MIT community. The initial design and implementation took place in January-May 2008. The site was officially launched in June 2008.

  • Project leader: Justin Anderson
  • Development and integration: Albert Chow, Alexandra Ellwood, Amon Horne, Sonya Huang, Pamela Luna, Brian Patt, David Tanner
  • User interface design: Eric Kim, Laura Watts
  • Team members: Andrew Munchbach, Joanna Proulx, Matthew Sullivan

We hope you find the site useful – and fun! Please send any feedback to mobiweb@mit.edu.
